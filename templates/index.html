Mallite is a platform where malls in Singapore and abroad are transformed into virtual malls!

As the upcoming new normal no longer mandates those who are infected by Covid-19 to be quarantined, we must find a solution to enforce intrinsic motivations in people such that they have more reasons to stay at home, and fewer reasons to visit shopping malls. Why is this important? Well, even though we are in a pandemic, some of us still have the incentive to go outside due to boredom or the need to break our home-based daily routines. Moreover, those of us who are ill - even with Covid-19 - sometimes still desire to step outdoors because of the very same reasons. Hence, should we be able to give ourselves minimal reasons to go outside, we can keep ourselves and our communities safe. This is where Mallite enters.

Furthermore, we hope to help small retailers (SMEs) to survive in the pandemic.
